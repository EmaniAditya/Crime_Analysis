Timeseries forecasting in simple words means to forecast or to predict the future value(eg-stock price) over a period of time.

There are some key concepts associated with time series forecasting :



  • Seasonality:In simple terms, Seasonality means repetition over time. For example, while predicting electricity bills will be higher every year in the summer season.

  • Trend : It tells increasing or decreasing trends over time in the data.
Fb Prophet library was launched by Facebook now meta, and it was built for time series analysis. It can automatically manage parameters related to seasonality and data stationarity.
